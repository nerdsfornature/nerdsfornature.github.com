Projects We Love

eCatch

eCatch is a tool developed by The Nature Conservancy that provides a simple way for fishermen to collect, map and share their fishing information.

Air Quality Egg

A community-led air quality sensing network that gives people a way to participate in the conversation about air quality.

Cal-Adapt

Cal-Adapt has been designed to provide access to the wealth of data and information that has been, and continues to be, produced by the State's scientific and research community.

iNaturalist

iNaturalist is a place where you can record what you see in nature, meet other nature lovers, and learn about the natural world.

Transit & Trails

Transit and Trails connects you to parks and open spaces by helping you Find, Plan and Share your outdoor adventures. You can locate trailheads near you (or any location) and we help you get there on public transportation or get the directions you need to walk, bike, or drive there.

growBot Garden

How might robotics and sensing technologies be used in support of local small-scale agriculture?

Surging Seas

Interactive map of sea level rise and flooding risks, with neighborhood zooms; risk timelines; city, county and state statistics; and links to factsheets, data downloads, action plans, embeddable widgets, and more.

Citizen Sensor

Citizen Sensor is DIY, wearable, reconfigurable sensor pack and data contextualization system that allows users to collect, share, and understand data using sensors recording environmental conditions such as carbon monoxide, light, noise pollution, and methane gas exposure among others.

LA Iconathon

The goal for the LA River Iconathon was to create new ways to visually communicate vital information about the river, and they succeeded in accomplishing that goal.

Dirty Energy Money

Dirty Energy Money is an interactive tool that tracks the flow of oil, gas and coal industry contributions to the US Congress.

PP Planter

PPlanter is a rapidly deployable, reconfigurable public urinal and sink that uses modular biofilters to treat urine and wastewater.
