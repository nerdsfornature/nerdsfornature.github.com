Nerds For Nature, Sequioa Audubon, and iNaturalist welcome you to Sugarloaf Mountain! This page should provide you with all the information you need to enjoy this nerdy nature adventure, please bookmark it on your mobile device for easy reference during the event.

The Only 5 Things You Really Need to Know

  1. Sign up for iNaturalist
  2. download the app: iPhone, Android
  3. Be at starting picnic area (directions here) at either 8:00 AM or 2:30 pm (or both times!) with fully charged smart phone
  4. Back at launch area at noon for morning shift or 6:30 pm for evening
  5. Be at the Wrap Session -- results, food, and prizes! -- at 12:00 for morning wrap or 6:30 for evening wrap. It's at a Roundtable Pizza near the park: 1304 West Hillsdale Boulevard San Mateo, CA 94403.

Many Other Things You Might Want to Know

If you have not registered via the Bioblitz EventBrite page, please do so now.

What You Should Bring

  • Water & snacks

  • Good walking shoes, hat, sun protection, extra layers, walking stick if you use them

  • Digital camera -- with zoom and macro lenses if you have them

  • Laptop -- if you need it to upload photos from your digital camera to the web at the wrap meeting. Don't leave it visible in your car though!

  • Smartphone -- iPhone or Android

  • Binoculars or a spotting scope if you've got one

  • Magnifying glass -- impromptu macro lens for smartphones, good for observing other details

  • Field guides

  • Device chargers -- we will have power strips at the wrap meeting

  • Spare batteries

Before You Arrive

  • Download the app

    • iPhone

    • Android

  • Get an iNaturalist account

  • Review the iNaturalist FAQ

  • Start the event day with fully-charged devices and keep Facebook and other extra usage to a minimum, so you can have power all day!
