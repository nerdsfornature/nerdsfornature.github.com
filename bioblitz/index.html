Grassroots Bioblitz: What is it?

A grassroots bioblitz is an event where local volunteers organize and conduct an intensive survey of the biological diversity of a park or other natural area. Participants record observations of as many different organisms as possible â€“ everything from algae to alligators. The event typically lasts for a few hours to a day, and observations are recorded through a smartphone application.
