Nerds For Nature, Save McLaren Park, the Bay Nature Institute and iNaturalist welcome you to John McLaren Park! This page should provide you with all the information you need to enjoy this nerdy nature adventure, please bookmark it on your mobile device for easy reference during the event.

The Only 5 Things You Really Need to Know

  1. Sign up for iNaturalist
  2. download the app: iPhone, Android
  3. Be at starting picnic area (Mansell and Visitacion, SF, CA) at noon with fully charged smart phone
  4. Back at launch area at 4 pm
  5. Be at Vis Valley Library (201 Leland Ave, SF, CA) at 4:30 for wrap, results prizes.

Many Other Things You Might Want to Know

If you have not registered via the Bioblitz EventBrite page, please do so now.

What You Should Bring

  • Water & snacks

  • Good walking shoes, hat, sun protection, extra layers

  • Digital camera -- with zoom and macro lenses if you have them

  • Laptop -- if you need it to upload photos from your digital camera to the web at the wrap meeting (we will be patrolling the parking lot so no worries about leaving it in your car during the main event)

  • Smartphone -- iPhone or Android

  • Binoculars

  • Magnifying glass -- impromptu macro lens for smartphones, good for observing other details

  • Field guides

  • Device chargers -- we will have power strips at the wrap meeting

  • Spare batteries

Before You Arrive

  • Download the app

    • iPhone

    • Android

  • Get an iNaturalist account

  • Review the iNaturalist FAQ

  • Start the event day with fully-charged devices and keep Facebook and other extra usage to a minimum, so you can have power all day!

Event Schedule

Getting Started

11:45 am

  • Lunch is Served - Free to all, bring a dish to share, donations not expected but gladly accepted.
  • When you arrive, stop by the check-in table to get a map and name tag, and to check in with your previous EventBrite registration (no ticket needed, just your name).
  • Meet & Greet your fellow nerds until:

12:30 pm

  • Welcome to McLaren Park - Ken McGary, SMP - 5 min
  • Welcome to N4N/Bay Nature Institute - Dan Rademacher, N4N/Bay Nature - 5 min
  • Introduction to iNaturalist - Ken-ichi Ueda and Scott Loarie, iNaturalist - 5 min
  • Zone Team Formation - Ken & Dan - 10 min
  • Final Q & A - All - 5 min
    Last chance to ask that nagging question in the back of your brain before the four teams scatter in different directions.

The Hunt Begins! (1-4 pm)

  • 1:00 pm: NorthWest and SouthWest teams move quickly towards their zones, then start counting species.
  • 1:15 pm: Latecomers can join the NorthEast and SouthEast teams, which will leave the starting area a little bit later.
  • 3:30 pm: All teams should be thinking about how they will arrive back at the starting point by 4:00 pm.
  • 4:00 pm: Meet back at the picnic area for the journey down to the library and wrap session. If you left a laptop in your car, now's the time to get it if you're not driving down to the library.

Wrap Session and Prizes

  • 4:30 pm: Wrap session, tallies, slideshow, and PRIZES at the Viz Valley Library
  • (red star on map, 201 Leland Ave, San Francisco, CA 94134)
  • 5:30 pm: Main blitz is done! Congrats and thanks! Off to dinner, and then for extra credit, come on back for:

Bonus Round!

  • 7:00 pm: Mothing with Jerry Powell and Liam! Back up at the picnic area where we started on Mansell
