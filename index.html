Nerds for Nature

From 2013 to 2017 or so, Nerds for Nature was an active group bringing together people with a passion for technology and the environment to understand, protect, and explore the natural world.

Since 2017, we've been mostly inactive, though things may start up again in New York City. Or in SF should new leadership emerge. Until then, this website serves as an archive of the cool stuff we made happen!
